8. Eight Golden Rules of Interface Design (Ben Shneiderman)

Strive for consistency

Enable shortcuts

Offer informative feedback

Design dialogs to yield closure

Offer error prevention and simple handling

Permit easy reversal of actions (undo)

Support internal locus of control (user in charge)

Reduce short-term memory load
